Transcriptome analysis

We aim to investigate compositional differences of protein between cancer and normal cells using various of NGS based transcriptome analysis.
We explore cell level changes of platelet in conjunction with cancer cell growth. We also analysis case/control clinical samples to identify biomarker or to understand disease mechanism.

Large-scale bio-health data analysis

We develop methods and analyze large scale public health data in conjunction with multi-omics data including genome, transcriptome, microbiome, metabolome and clinical data to predict onset of disease.
We have focused on developing prediction model of future onset of type 2 diabetes and female obesity.

Developing label-free cancer liquid biopsy

We have identified dozens of cancer-specific transcriptional and transitional profile in platelets.
By using this spectral characteristics of these profile, our goal is to develop a technology that enables rapid cancer diagnosis withouth the need for molecular amplification or fluorescent markers.
